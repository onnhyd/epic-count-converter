About Us

We are three B.Tech students from the Department of Jute and Fibre Technology at the University of Calcutta, formerly known as the Institute of Jute Technology. Our collaborative effort led to the creation of a simplified tool designed to convert yarn counts seamlessly. The primary goal of this project was to develop a user-friendly tool capable of converting any yarn count to another, streamlining and enhancing the efficiency of this process.
